This is me, briefly

According to the Myers-Briggs test, I’m a Mediator (INFP): intuitive, creative, and moved by high ideals. I agree, and I would add obsessed with details and incredibly curious.

I graduated in Economics and Management, but I soon after understood that I truly feel fulfilled as a UX/UI Designer, because I can combine creative ideas, design guidelines and psychological principles to meet the users' needs.

As a good multipotentialite, I’m also getting keen on Frontend Development. I must say that being able to design and code my own things is incredibly satisfying.
